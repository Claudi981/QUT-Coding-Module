Claudia O'Hara

About

I am an experienced clinician with a passion for working with children and young people. Over the next four years I will be undertaking my PhD which aims to understand the occupational/participation challenges of children and young people who have experienced Early Life Adversity (ELA). The research will be conducted from an Occupational Therapy perspective; specifically investigating the impact of ELA on occupational participation, and how this can be helped by the use of occupation as a method of increasing participation. Essentially, moving away from a psychology heavy approach and toward an occupational approach that can help the children and young people to engage in daily activities that support their growth and development and lessen the chance of increasing the developmental gap between them and their peers without experience of ELA.
